PROMPT 3: Standard Change-Detection Classification
============================================================
You are analyzing year-over-year changes in SEC 10-K risk factor disclosures.

Risk factor from {year_a}:
{summary_a}

Risk factor from {year_b}:
{summary_b}

Classify the change as exactly one of: ESCALATED, DE-ESCALATED, UNCHANGED.
- ESCALATED: the risk grew more severe, broader, or more prominently disclosed
- DE-ESCALATED: the risk shrank, was mitigated, or received less emphasis
- UNCHANGED: the risk language and severity are essentially the same

Respond with only the label.
